👋, I'm David.

I'm a 3rd Year Electronic and Computer Engineering student in National University of Ireland, Galway. I'm currently interning at [Intercom](https://intercom.io).

I'm interested in building great software, most particularly in the areas of Education, TV & Film and Space; and the ensuing companies/cultures of these pursuits.

In the summer of 2015, I setup [Student Startup](http://studentsstartup.com), a programme to introduce Irish students to startups. Our weeklong event saw 15 students (based in the offices of [Dublin's Commissioner for Startups](http://startupdublin.com/)), meet with Dublin's startup heros, learn from individual mentors and tour startup offices. A 150 person event held in [Dublin's Science Gallery](https://dublin.sciencegallery.com/) featured a talk from [Paul Adams](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hfS4oZrX2Ew) and a panel on what it takes to [Grow a Startup in Ireland](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=INvXNuxCreg).

You can find some of my open sourced work (including this site) on [GitHub](https://github.com/daibhin).
