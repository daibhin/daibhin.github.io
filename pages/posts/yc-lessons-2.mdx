**Have confidence in yourself**. The belief it will all work out is almost as important as having a plan.

**Think a little differently**. Have a slightly askew version of reality. Try something “interesting” and different. Be novel in your approach. Wacky probably won’t win in the end but push yourself to think slightly beyond the norm. Two steps outside the box of normality is where the best ideas flourish.
