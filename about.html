👋, I'm David.

I'm a 3rd Year Electronic and Computer Engineering student in National University of Ireland, Galway. I'm currently interning at Intercom.

I'm interested in building great software, most particularly in the areas of Education, TV & Film and Space; and the ensuing companies/cultures of these pursuits.

In the summer of 2015, I setup Student Startup, a programme to introduce Irish students to startups. Our weeklong event saw 15 students (based in the offices of Dublin's Commissioner for Startups), meet with Dublin's startup heros, learn from individual mentors and tour startup offices. A 150 person event held in Dublin's Science Gallery featured a talk from Paul Adams and a panel on what it takes to Grow a Startup in Ireland.

You can find some of my open sourced work (including this site) on GitHub.

Feel free to contact me on LinkedIn, Twitter, or preferably send me a note at d.newell1@outlook.com.
